Console | Edit your artist and album rankings

An introduction

Hiya!! Welcome to your editor. Here you can add artists, albums, rankings and manage your data.

Adding your first album

Use the "+ Add Artist" button to add a new artist. An artist can be removed with the "-" button and albums can be added with the "+" on the right of "Albums".

Manage an album

You can name an added album anything, add your rating in a 1-100 number and the album cover, however there is one thing you need to be aware of!

Album cover issues, also known as CORS

When choosing your albums' covers, make sure to use good and trusted sources. Many image sources block Cross-Origin Resource Sharing, or better known as CORS.

This leads to album images not being visible when you export them. This is not a big issue, but one to be aware of.

Recommended album cover sources include: Spotify, Wikipedia, various streaming services. Do NOT use: magazines, album review pages, news websites.

How do I change album order?

We suggest you keep your artists' albums in the release order. On the left of each album, use the Grab button to drag albums up and down to change the order.

Managing several artists

So artists are automatically sorted on the website, so there is NO need to worry about sorting artists or making a certain order. On the home page, using the "Sort:" dropdown, you can choose between Alphabetical and an Average Rating artist sorting.

How does sorting work?

Alphabetical: your artists are sorted from A to Z based on the English alphabet.

By ratings: your artists are sorted based on a simple and fair calculation system. The artists albums' rankings (above 0) are all put into artist points.However, you can always change how it is being calculated in your settings.

Editor errors

If you are experiencing errors with the 'Save' function, we are probably working on an update to our authentication and/or saving system. Please try again later- a fix is soon to come!
